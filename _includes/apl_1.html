Machine Learning for Global Road Transportation Emissions Estimation

I led a cross-disciplinary team to estimate road transportation emissions for 500 global cities as part of the Climate TRACE coalition. We built and deployed machine learning models on the Microsoft Planetary Computer using Dask and Xarray to efficiently process large-scale Sentinel-2 imagery. Our results were presented at leading conferences, including the CVPR EarthVision Workshop and the NeurIPS Tackling Climate Change with Machine Learning Workshop
